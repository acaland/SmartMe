VALUE

DEFINITION

AccDay

Daily average accuracy (AccDay), defined as the percentage of auditory stimuli responded to within the given time (Treact) on a working day.

ReactTimeDay

Daily average reaction time (ReactTimeDay), defined as the reaction time to auditory stimuli responded to on a working day.

Avg 7 days

Indicates the average trend of the value over the past 7 working days.

Avg 30 days

Indicates the average trend of the value over the past 30 working days.
